Hi, I'm Martin

Who am I?

My name is Martin. I am 22 years old and I come from Estonia. Currently I am studying and working in Copenhagen, Denmark.

What do I do?

For the most part of my career I have been a self-employed photographer/videographer and I have worked with various companies creating content for advertisements, social media etc.

However at one point I felt that all of this was not fulfilling enough, so I decided to combine all of my skills and become a multimedia designer/web developer or something along those lines.

What have I learned?

During the first semester at KEA we had 4 main topics:

  1. Basic Web
  2. Basic Content
  3. Basic Animation
  4. Basic UX / UI

These topics gave me a lot of new knowledge and experience, but also gave me so much inspiration to keep learning so that I could one day become a real web developer.
